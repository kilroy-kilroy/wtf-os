Revised Phased Rollout — Videos Earlier

Demo videos move to Phase 1 alongside the how-to guides

PHASE 1 — Weeks 1-2
How-To Guides + Demo Videos

Write guides and record demos in parallel. The video recording is basically the same voice-walkthrough you'd do to draft the guide — so do both at once.

Week 1: Call Lab + Discovery Lab
For each tool:
1. Screen-record yourself using the Pro tool (5-8 min)
2. Use that same voice recording to draft the how-to guide
3. Edit guide, publish on timkilroy.com
4. Edit video, upload to YouTube, embed in guide
5. Cut 2-3 social clips (30-60 sec) from each video

The video IS your voice draft — one recording, two assets.
Week 2: Visibility Lab + Polish
Same process for Visibility Lab:
1. Screen-record Pro tool walkthrough
2. Draft guide from recording
3. Publish guide + video
4. Cut social clips

Also in Week 2:
• Update /agency-tools page to link all 3 guides
• Publish social clips from Week 1 videos
• First LinkedIn posts driving to guides
Phase 1 output: 3 how-to guides (published) + 3 demo videos (YouTube + embedded) + 6-9 social clips + updated /agency-tools hub
PHASE 2 — Weeks 3-4
Problem-Solution Blog Posts

Now that guides and videos exist, start publishing keyword-targeted posts that link back to them. 2-3 posts/week.

Call Lab posts
• "5 Signs You're Losing Deals on Discovery Calls"
• "What Top Agency Sellers Do Differently on Calls"
• "Sales Call Scoring: What Metrics Actually Matter"
Discovery Lab posts
• "Pre-Call Research: The 15-Minute Process"
• "How to Research a Prospect Without Being Creepy"
• "Why Your Discovery Calls Feel Like Interrogations"
Visibility Lab posts
• "Is Your Agency Invisible? How to Find Out"
• "Agency SEO: Why Competitors Show Up and You Don't"
• "Market Visibility Audit: A Step-by-Step Guide"
Phase 2 output: 6-10 blog posts (published) + each one amplified to LinkedIn + newsletter
PHASE 3 — Weeks 5-6
Pillar Expansion + WTF Assessment + Cross-Linking

Same as before — expand guides into full pillar pages, add WTF Assessment cluster, cross-link everything.

• Guides grow to 3,000-5,000 words with FAQ, comparison tables, testimonials
• WTF Assessment guide + walkthrough video + 6-8 supporting posts
• Cross-linking sprint across all content
• /agency-tools becomes the definitive hub
What changed from v1:
  • Videos moved to Phase 1 — recorded alongside guides, same walkthrough creates both
  • Social clips cut immediately — 6-9 clips from 3 videos, dripped out during Phase 2
  • Blog posts become Phase 2 — they link back to guides that already have videos embedded
  • Phase 3 unchanged — pillar expansion + WTF Assessment + cross-linking
