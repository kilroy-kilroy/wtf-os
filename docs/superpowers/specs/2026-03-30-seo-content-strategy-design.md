# SEO & Content Strategy for WTF Growth OS Tools

**Date:** 2026-03-30
**Status:** Approved
**Author:** Tim Kilroy + Claude

## Overview

Build SEO authority for Call Lab Pro, Discovery Lab Pro, Visibility Lab Pro, and WTF Assessment through an integrated content strategy combining how-to guides, demo videos, and keyword-targeted blog posts — all published on timkilroy.com under the Tim Kilroy personal brand.

## Context

- **Current state:** Landing pages exist at timkilroy.com/call-lab, /call-lab-pro, /discovery-lab, /discovery-lab-pro, /visibility-lab, /visibility-lab-pro, /wtf-assessment. Existing blog content on timkilroy.com and agencyinnercircle.com. Tools hub at timkilroy.com/agency-tools.
- **Gap:** Strong word-of-mouth and social (LinkedIn) presence, but minimal organic search traffic. "Strangers searching for solutions" can't find the tools.
- **Goal:** Capture organic search traffic from agency owners/founders (65%) and sales leaders looking for coaching tools (35%).
- **Content capacity:** High — voice-first workflow (record → Claude drafts → edit → publish), 2-4 pieces/week.
- **Newsletter:** Growing via Optin Growth partnership.

## Strategy: Hybrid Pillar + Problem-Solution

Build 4 pillar pages (one per tool category) as long-term SEO assets, while publishing problem-solution blog posts immediately for quick wins. How-to guides serve as pillar seeds — written first, expanded later.

### Content Architecture

```
timkilroy.com/agency-tools (master hub)
├── /call-lab-guide (pillar) ← 8-12 blog posts
│   ├── → /call-lab (free tool CTA)
│   ├── → /call-lab-pro (upgrade CTA)
│   ├── → YouTube demo video (embedded)
│   └── cross-links → other pillar pages
├── /discovery-lab-guide (pillar) ← 8-12 blog posts
│   └── (same pattern)
├── /visibility-lab-guide (pillar) ← 8-12 blog posts
│   └── (same pattern)
└── /wtf-assessment-guide (pillar) ← 6-8 blog posts
    └── (same pattern)
```

**Linking rules:**
- Every blog post links to its parent pillar
- Every pillar links to the hub (/agency-tools)
- Pillars cross-link to each other where relevant
- Blog posts cross-link to 1-2 related posts in the same or adjacent cluster
- Primary CTA is always the free tool; Pro upgrade is secondary

### Amplification Layer

Every piece of content gets distributed through 3 channels:
1. **Newsletter** — each post becomes a newsletter edition via Optin Growth
2. **LinkedIn** — each post becomes a LinkedIn post with key insight + link
3. **Agency Inner Circle** — cross-post relevant content to community

## Phased Rollout (6 Weeks)

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1-2: How-To Guides + Demo Videos

Write 3 how-to guides and record 3 demo videos in parallel. The video recording IS the voice draft — one screen recording creates both assets.

**Week 1: Call Lab + Discovery Lab**
- Screen-record Pro tool walkthrough (5-8 min each)
- Use recording to draft how-to guide via Claude
- Edit and publish guide on timkilroy.com
- Edit video, upload to YouTube, embed in guide
- Cut 2-3 social clips (30-60 sec) per video

**Week 2: Visibility Lab + Polish**
- Same process for Visibility Lab
- Update /agency-tools to link all 3 guides prominently
- Begin posting social clips from Week 1 videos
- First LinkedIn posts driving to guides

**Phase 1 output:** 3 how-to guides (published), 3 demo videos (YouTube + embedded), 6-9 social clips, updated /agency-tools hub.

#### How-To Guide Structure

Each guide covers both Lite and Pro versions (~2,000-2,500 words initially):

**Call Lab Guide — "How to Analyze Your Sales Calls with AI"**
- What call analysis reveals
- Step-by-step: using Call Lab Lite
- What Pro adds (coaching, patterns, trend tracking)
- Reading your scores
- CTA → try Call Lab free

**Discovery Lab Guide — "How to Do Pre-Call Research That Wins Deals"**
- Why most reps wing it
- Step-by-step: using Discovery Lab Lite
- What Pro adds (deep intel, strategic questions)
- Using briefs in your calls
- CTA → try Discovery Lab free

**Visibility Lab Guide — "How to Audit Your Agency's Market Visibility"**
- What market visibility means
- Step-by-step: using Visibility Lab Lite
- What Pro adds (tracking, trends)
- Acting on your results
- CTA → try Visibility Lab free

#### Demo Video Structure

One per Pro tool (5-8 minutes, screen recording + narration):
- Call Lab Pro: Upload a call → show analysis → walk through scores and coaching → show pattern recognition
- Discovery Lab Pro: Enter a prospect → show research generating → walk through brief → show pre-call strategy
- Visibility Lab Pro: Run an analysis → show results → walk through positioning → show tracking and trends

**YouTube optimization:** Title format "How to [Benefit] with [Tool Name] — Demo". 200+ word description with keywords and links. Chapter markers. Thumbnail: face + tool screenshot + benefit text.

**Social clips:** 2-3 per video, focus on "aha moments" (score reveal, surprising insight, before/after). Caption: pose problem → "here's what I found". CTA: "Full demo + free tool → link in comments".

### Phase 2 — Weeks 3-4: Problem-Solution Blog Posts

Publish 6-10 keyword-targeted posts (2-3/week). Each links back to its parent pillar guide.

**Blog post template (800-1,500 words):**
- Hook — the problem (2-3 sentences)
- The insight / framework / answer
- How to apply this
- CTA: "Want help? Try [Tool Name]" → free tool link
- Links back to parent pillar page
- 1-2 cross-links to related posts
- Schema markup: Article

Each post is amplified: LinkedIn post + newsletter edition.

### Phase 3 — Weeks 5-6: Pillar Expansion + WTF Assessment + Cross-Linking

**Expand guides into full pillar pages (3,000-5,000 words):**
- Add "why this matters" section with data/stories
- Embed demo video
- Add FAQ section (targets featured snippets)
- Add table of contents
- Link to all supporting blog posts
- Add Lite vs Pro comparison section
- Add testimonials/case studies if available

**WTF Assessment content cluster:**
- Write "How to Diagnose Why Your Agency Isn't Growing" guide
- Record assessment walkthrough video
- 6-8 supporting blog posts (see keyword cluster below)
- CTA → take the free WTF Assessment

**Cross-linking sprint:** Go back through all content and wire up internal links per the linking rules above.

**Pillar page template:**
- `<title>` [Head Term]: The Complete Guide for Agencies | Tim Kilroy
- `<h1>` The Complete Guide to [Topic]
- `<h2>` Table of contents
- `<h2>` Why [Topic] matters for agencies
- `<h2>` How to [do the thing] — step by step
- `<h2>` [Tool Name]: How it works (+ demo video)
- `<h2>` Free vs Pro: What you get
- `<h2>` FAQ (targets featured snippets)
- `<h2>` Related articles (links to cluster posts)
- Schema markup: Article + FAQ + HowTo

### Ongoing — Week 7+

- 2 posts/week, alternating across tool clusters
- Monthly pillar refresh (new supporting post links, fresh data, new FAQs)
- Quarterly video update (re-record demos when tools get new features)

## Content Production Workflow

Tim's voice-first process:
1. Voice-record walkthrough or thoughts on topic
2. Claude drafts article from recording transcript
3. Tim edits for voice, accuracy, and insight
4. Publish on timkilroy.com
5. Create LinkedIn post with key insight + link
6. Send as newsletter edition via Optin Growth
7. Cross-post to Agency Inner Circle where relevant

For demo videos, steps 1-2 happen simultaneously — the screen recording IS the voice draft.
