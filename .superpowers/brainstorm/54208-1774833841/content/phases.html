Phased Rollout — 6 Week Plan

Starting from your existing timkilroy.com/agency-tools page

PHASE 1 — Weeks 1-2
How-To Guides (Pillar Seeds)

Write 3 how-to guides — these are your most valuable content pieces and they become pillar seeds. Each guide covers both Lite and Pro versions of the tool.

📞 Call Lab Guide
"How to Analyze Your Sales Calls with AI"

• What call analysis reveals
• Step-by-step: using Call Lab Lite
• What Pro adds (coaching, patterns)
• Reading your scores
• ~2,000-2,500 words
🔍 Discovery Lab Guide
"How to Do Pre-Call Research That Wins Deals"

• Why most reps wing it
• Step-by-step: using Discovery Lab Lite
• What Pro adds (deep intel, strategy)
• Using briefs in your calls
• ~2,000-2,500 words
👁️ Visibility Lab Guide
"How to Audit Your Agency's Market Visibility"

• What market visibility means
• Step-by-step: using Visibility Lab Lite
• What Pro adds (tracking, trends)
• Acting on your results
• ~2,000-2,500 words
Your workflow: Voice-record yourself walking through each tool → Claude drafts → you edit → publish on timkilroy.com
Also in Phase 1: Update /agency-tools to link to each guide prominently
PHASE 2 — Weeks 2-4
Problem-Solution Blog Posts + Demo Videos

Start publishing 2-3 posts/week targeting specific search queries. Simultaneously record demo videos for each Pro tool.

Blog Posts (6-10 total in this phase)
Example topics by cluster:

Call Lab cluster:
• "5 Signs You're Losing Deals on Discovery Calls"
• "What Top Agency Sellers Do Differently on Calls"
• "Sales Call Scoring: What Metrics Actually Matter"

Discovery Lab cluster:
• "Pre-Call Research: The 15-Minute Process That Wins Deals"
• "How to Research a Prospect Without Being Creepy"

Visibility Lab cluster:
• "Is Your Agency Invisible? How to Find Out"
• "Agency SEO: Why Your Competitors Show Up and You Don't"

Each post: 800-1,500 words, links back to the relevant guide
Demo Videos (3 total)
One per Pro tool. Screen recording + your narration:

Call Lab Pro Demo (5-8 min)
• Upload a call → show analysis happening
• Walk through scores and coaching
• Show pattern recognition over time

Discovery Lab Pro Demo (5-8 min)
• Enter a prospect → show research generating
• Walk through brief sections
• Show how to use it before a call

Visibility Lab Pro Demo (5-8 min)
• Run an analysis → show results
• Walk through market positioning
• Show tracking and trends

Host on YouTube, embed in guides + pillar pages
Amplification: Every blog post → LinkedIn post → newsletter edition. Demo videos → social clips (30-60 sec).
PHASE 3 — Weeks 4-6
Pillar Expansion + WTF Assessment + Cross-Linking

Expand your 3 how-to guides into full pillar pages. Add WTF Assessment content. Wire up all cross-links.

Expand Guides → Pillar Pages
Each guide grows from ~2,500 to 3,000-5,000 words:
• Add "why this matters" section with data/stories
• Embed demo video
• Add FAQ section (targets featured snippets)
• Add table of contents
• Link to all supporting blog posts
• Add comparison section (Lite vs Pro)
• Add testimonials/case studies if available
WTF Assessment Content
• Write "How to Evaluate Your Sales Process" guide
• Record assessment walkthrough video
• 6-8 supporting posts:
  – "Are You Winging Your Sales Process?"
  – "Sales Process Audit: Where Agencies Lose Deals"
  – "The 5 Stages of Agency Sales Maturity"
• CTA → take the free WTF Assessment
Cross-linking sprint: Go back through all content and add internal links. Every post links to its pillar. Pillars link to each other where relevant. /agency-tools becomes the master hub linking to all 4 pillars.
ONGOING — Week 7+
Sustain + Compound
2 posts/week
Continue problem-solution posts, alternating across tool clusters
Monthly pillar refresh
Update pillar pages with new supporting post links, fresh data, new FAQs
Quarterly video update
Re-record demos when tools get new features, add customer story videos
