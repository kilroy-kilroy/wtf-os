On-Page SEO Structure
Pillar Page Template
<title> [Head Term]: The Complete Guide for Agencies | Tim Kilroy
<h1> The Complete Guide to [Topic]
<h2> Table of contents
<h2> Why [Topic] matters for agencies
<h2> How to [do the thing] — step by step
<h2> [Tool Name]: How it works (+ demo video)
<h2> Free vs Pro: What you get
<h2> FAQ (targets featured snippets)
<h2> Related articles (links to cluster posts)

• Schema markup: Article + FAQ + HowTo
• Meta description includes head term + CTA
• Internal links to 8-12 supporting posts
Blog Post Template
<title> [Long-tail keyword] | Tim Kilroy
<h1> [Engaging headline with keyword]
<p> Hook — the problem (2-3 sentences)
<h2> The insight / framework / answer
<h2> How to apply this
<h2> Want help? Try [Tool Name] (CTA)

• 800-1,500 words
• Links back to parent pillar page
• 1-2 cross-links to related blog posts
• CTA → free tool (not Pro directly)
• Schema markup: Article

Rule: Every blog post links to its parent pillar. Every pillar links to the hub (/agency-tools). Pillars cross-link to each other. Blog posts cross-link to 1-2 related posts in the same or adjacent cluster.
Demo Video SEO
YouTube Optimization
• Title: "How to [Benefit] with [Tool Name] — Demo"
• Description: 200+ words, include target keywords, link to pillar page and free tool
• Tags: tool name, problem keywords, "agency sales"
• Chapters: mark key sections for jump navigation
• Thumbnail: your face + tool screenshot + benefit text
Social Clips
• 2-3 clips per video (30-60 sec each)
• Focus on "aha moments" — the score reveal, a surprising insight, the before/after
• Caption: pose the problem → "here's what I found"
• CTA: "Full demo + free tool → link in comments"
• Post on LinkedIn, repurpose for X/shorts
