**2\. The Charlie Brown Detector** Narrow, cheap, sharable. Paste your site copy, it flags every "results-focused / extension of your team / 360-degree / customer-centric" wah-wah phrase and tells you what you're actually trying to say underneath. Almost a free tool. Pure top-of-funnel, screams your POV, and it's *funny*, which sells. Honestly might be better as a free lead-gen toy than a paid product.

**4\. The Extraction Interview (async)** The differentiator made into a product. You don't show up live, but the *structure* of your live magic does: a sequenced set of questions (favorite clients and why, what they got that they didn't expect, where you live in their ecosystem, what makes you swear) that the user records or types answers to, and the tool assembles the raw VVV material. This is the one closest to what you actually do, and the one a prompt pack can't copy, because the value is in the *questions*, not the generation.
