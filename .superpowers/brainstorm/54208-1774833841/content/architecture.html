How content flows:
PILLAR PAGES (4 total)
Definitive 3,000-5,000 word guides. Each covers:
• What the problem is (why you need this)
• How the tool solves it
• How-to guide embedded
• Demo video embedded
• Links to all supporting posts
• CTA → free tool / Pro upgrade
SUPPORTING POSTS (30-44 total)
Individual 800-1,500 word posts targeting specific keywords:
• "How to analyze a sales call" → links to Call Lab pillar
• "Pre-call research checklist" → links to Discovery Lab pillar
• "Agency market visibility" → links to Visibility Lab pillar
• Each solves one problem, CTA → relevant free tool
• Cross-links between related posts
Amplification layer:
Newsletter
Each post → newsletter edition via Optin Growth
Social (LinkedIn)
Each post → LinkedIn post with key insight + link
Agency Inner Circle
Cross-post relevant content to community
Key design decisions in this architecture:
  • Everything lives on timkilroy.com — one domain accumulates all SEO authority
  • How-to guides ARE the pillar seeds — you start writing guides immediately, they evolve into full pillars
  • Demo videos embed into pillars — not standalone YouTube-first content
  • Free tools are the CTA — every post drives to a free tool, Pro upgrade is secondary
  • Cross-linking is structural — posts link to pillars, pillars link to posts, tools cross-reference each other
